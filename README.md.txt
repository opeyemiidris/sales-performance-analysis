# 📊 Sales Performance Analysis

This project analyzes sales data from a fictional retail store to uncover trends, regional performance, product success, and sales rep efficiency.

---

## 📁 Files Included

- `sales_data.xlsx` – Raw dataset (500 records of daily sales)
- `Sales_Analysis.py` – Python script with analysis (for use in Jupyter or VS Code)
- Visuals generated via matplotlib & seaborn

---

## 📌 Objective

To answer key business questions such as:
- Which region has the highest total sales?
- What products are generating the most revenue?
- How do monthly sales trends vary?
- Who are the top-performing sales reps?

---

## 🛠 Tools Used

- **Python**: Pandas, Matplotlib, Seaborn  
- **Excel**: Data preview and export  
- *(Power BI version coming soon)*

---

## 📈 Key Insights (Example)

- The **West** region had the highest total sales in the period.
- **Laptops** were the top-selling product in terms of revenue.
- Sales peaked in **March and April**.
- Sales reps **Ethan** and **Diana** were consistently top performers.

---

## ✅ How to Run

1. Open `sales_data.xlsx` to view the raw data.
2. Run `Sales_Analysis.py` in Jupyter Notebook or any Python environment.
3. Explore trends and use plots to drive business decisions.
